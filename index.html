IMPORTANT SCRIPTING DETAILS

  • Duration is a Hard Limit: Under 60 seconds for Quick Tips (QT) and 2:30 maximum for ICP Demos. Failure to adhere to these lengths kills conversion.
  • Voiceover Only: No on-screen human talent is permitted in any video. This is mandatory for global localization velocity and cost control.
  • Immediate Problem Hook: The first 10 seconds of every video must state the ICP’s high-stakes pain point (e.g., fines, downtime, wasted time).
  • Screen Recording Focus: The visual core of all 24 videos must be the EcoOnline UI showing the solution in action. The product is the star.
  • Clear, Single CTA: Every script must end with a single, explicit Call-to-Action (e.g., "Book a demo," "Download the app," "Read the full guide").
